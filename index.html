Mushroom Cheese Sandwich

Describe Mushroom Cheese Sandwich.

Mushroom Cheese Sandwich is a good source of protein,carbohydrate,vitamins and minerals. It is easy to make and has fiber value with rich taste of cheese. My mother used to cook it for me and it is one of my favourite dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Kissan Tomato Ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander, chopped
  • 4 slices Whole wheat bread
  • 2 cheese slices
  • salt to taste
  • 30 gms cheese, grated
  • 30 gms carrot, grated
  • Mix 30 gms bell peppers, chopped

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet set over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook 2-3 minutes, or until soft and fragrant. Add the mushrooms and cook 4-5 minutes, or until they begin to soften. Add Kissan Tomato Ketchup. Cook for about 2 minutes. Stir in the parsley and season with salt and pepper. Add grated cheese and mix well.
  2. Top 2 slices of bread with one slice each of cheddar. Top with the mushroom mixture and cover with the remaining bread.
  3. Cook sandwiches while covered over medium heat for 4 minutes on each side, or until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
